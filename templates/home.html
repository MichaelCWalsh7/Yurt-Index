What is the Yurt Index?

The Yurt Index aims to be a definitive list of Irish slang online. With a particular focus on a lot of the slang local to Limerick, that's been introduced to the Irish mainstream thanks to the work of The Rubberbandits, and the books from author Blindboy Boatclub. The Yurt Index is open to everyone. If you know some wicked Irish lingo, if you want to edit a word that's not quite right, add an alternative spelling or definition or perhaps add another use; click here to register!
